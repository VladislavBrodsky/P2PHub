# Pintopay Knowledge Base Checklist

## 1. General & Mission
- **Q**: What is Pintopay?
  - **A**: Pintopay is a borderless financial platform that bridges the gap between traditional banking and the digital economy. We offer virtual and physical crypto cards, P2P payments, and a partner network that rewards you for growth.
- **Q**: Is Pintopay a bank?
  - **A**: No, we are a financial technology company. We provide the interface and tools to manage your digital assets and spend them globally, but we are not a traditional bank with physical branches.
- **Q**: Is it safe?
  - **A**: Absoluteled. We use banking-grade encryption, 3D Secure for cards, and non-custodial wallet options. Your assets are your own.

## 2. Cards (Virtual & Physical)
- **Q**: How do I get a card?
  - **A**: Go to the "Cards" tab in the app, select Virtual or Physical, and click "Issue Card". You'll need to pay the small issuance fee.
- **Q**: What crypto can I use to top up?
  - **A**: We accept USDT (TRC20/ERC20) and TON. Top-ups are instant.
- **Q**: Does the card work with Apple Pay / Google Pay?
  - **A**: Yes! Our Virtual Cards are Apple Pay and Google Pay ready immediately after issuance.
- **Q**: What are the limits?
  - **A**: Basic cards have a $5,000 monthly limit. completion of KYC level 2 increases this to $50,000. Platinum partners have limits up to $200,000.
- **Q**: Can I withdraw cash?
  - **A**: Yes, with the Physical Card you can withdraw cash at any Mastercard-compatible ATM worldwide.

## 3. Partner Network & Earnings
- **Q**: How do I earn money?
  - **A**: Share your unique invite link. You earn commissions on card issuance fees (up to 30%) and a percentage of every top-up your network makes (up to 0.5% lifetime).
- **Q**: What is the $1/minute strategy?
  - **A**: This is our elite goal. By building a network of 5,000+ active users who transact daily, your passive stream hits $1 per minute ($1440/day).
- **Q**: When do I get paid?
  - **A**: Earnings are credited to your Agent Wallet instantly. You can withdraw to your main wallet or crypto address at any time.
- **Q**: What is XP?
  - **A**: XP (Experience Points) determines your Rank. You get XP for invites, daily checks-in, and your network's activity. Higher Rank = Higher Commissions.

## 4. Technical / Troubleshooting
- **Q**: My top-up didn't arrive.
  - **A**: Check the blockchain transaction hash (TXID). If it's confirmed on-chain, please wait 10-15 minutes. If still missing, send the TXID to support.
- **Q**: Card declined?
  - **A**: Ensure you have sufficient balance. Check if "International Transactions" are enabled in card settings. Some merchants require 3D Secure verification.
- **Q**: How do I reset my PIN?
  - **A**: You can reset your card PIN securely inside the "Cards" tab settings.

## 5. Account & Security
- **Q**: Can I have multiple accounts?
  - **A**: No. One person, one account. We strictly ban multi-accounting / bots to protect the partner ecosystem.
- **Q**: Do I need KYC?
  - **A**: For basic features, minimal info is needed. For higher limits and physical cards, standard Identity Verification (Passport/ID) is required.
